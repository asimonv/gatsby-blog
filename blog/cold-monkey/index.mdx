「初しぐれ猿も小蓑をほしげ也 <br/>
&nbsp;&nbsp;はつしぐれさるもこみのをほしげなり」 <br/>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;_"hatsu shigure saru mo komino o hoshige nari"_

<br />

the first cold shower <br />
even the monkey seems to want <br />
a little coat of straw
